Brain Tumor Segmentation using U-Net

This project involves utilizing the U-Net architecture to perform brain tumor segmentation on MRI images from the BraTS 2020 dataset. The goal was to classify each pixel into categories such as Normal, Necrotic and Non-Enhancing Tumor Core, Peritumoral Edema, and GD-enhancing tumor. It includes training models with both a pre-trained MobileNetV2 encoder and a model trained from scratch, demonstrating superior performance in accuracy and generalization with the latter approach.
