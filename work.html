Asteria Aerospace

During my final year, I worked at Asteria Aerospace as an Embedded Software Intern from June to December 2020. I developed a solution for autonomous indoor navigation in the absence of GPS signals for Quadcopter drones, with visual-inertial odometry and SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithms.

Using an Intel T265 camera and NVIDIA Jetson Xavier NX edge devices, I engineered a system to process and transmit spatial data from the camera to the simulation software utilizing ROS as an abstraction layer between the camera hardware and the simulation software.
